About Me

My journey in design began with a Bachelor's in Industrial/Product Design from MIT Institute of Design, where I cultivated critical thinking and aesthetic sensibilities. Currently pursuing a M.A in UX/UI Design at New York Institute of Technology, I'm refining my skills in responsive web design and user research. With a fusion of 3D modeling, manufacturing optimization, and UX/UI methodologies, I aim to create user-centric solutions that marry form and function seamlessly. At SureView Instruments LLP, I thrived as an Assistant UX Designer, excelling in problem-solving and collaborative teamwork. My contributions to web and mobile applications for industrial automation were marked by iterative refinement and a keen understanding of user needs. Prior, as a Product Design Intern at MICROGRADE TOOLS, I optimized manufacturing processes and contributed to innovative product design through CAD and rapid prototyping techniques. My journey is driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to creating impactful products that enhance user experiences. I look forward to leveraging my diverse skill set and collaborating with fellow designers and stakeholders to tackle real-world challenges in the realms of UX/UI and Industrial Design..
